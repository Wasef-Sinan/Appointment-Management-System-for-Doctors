Appointment Management System for Doctors

Welcome to the Doctor Appointment Management System

Project Objective

The primary objective of this system is to develop a user-friendly, web-based application that simplifies and automates the process of managing doctor appointments. Patients can register, view available doctors, book appointments, and receive an automatically generated appointment receipt. Administrators can efficiently manage doctors, patients, appointments, and payment records through a centralized MySQL database, ensuring secure data management, reducing manual paperwork, and improving the overall efficiency of appointment scheduling.
